About the tour:

Sikkim is a wonderland for those who love peace, nature, and spirituality. Sikkim, surrounded by the abode of clouds and peeping peaks of the Himalayas, is an ideal location to witness the beauty of Mount Kanchenjunga, the world's third highest peak. Take this trip to the renowned hill station of Gangtok, where you will get a chance to visit the colourful monasteries, cascading waterfalls, and pristine high altitude lakes with stunning mountain views all around. Remember to catch the misty mountain views with your camera lens.
